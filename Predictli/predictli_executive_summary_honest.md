**The Problem:**
Recruitment agencies have 10,000+ old applicants sitting in their ATS doing nothing. These dormant candidates represent $2-3M in wasted placement fees per agency. When a new job opens, recruiters post to job boards and pay agencies instead of reactivating their own goldmine.

**The Solution:**
Predictli sits on top of any ATS (Greenhouse, Lever, JobAdder) and continuously reactivates dormant candidates via WhatsApp/SMS, runs AI interviews, and uses XGBoost to learn which matches actually result in placements. It's like having a 24/7 recruiter who never forgets anyone.

**Why Now:**
- WhatsApp Business API is mature and cheap ($0.005/message)
- GPT-4/Gemini can conduct convincing micro-interviews
- ATS APIs are standardized (Greenhouse, Lever, etc.)
- COVID normalized AI-first hiring (less stigma)
